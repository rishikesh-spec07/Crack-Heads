# Information Security Management System (ISMS) Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes the framework for managing information security within the organization.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party service providers who have access to organizational information systems and data.

## 3. Information Security Objectives
The organization is committed to:
- Protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets
- Ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations
- Minimizing business risks related to information security

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
### 4.1 Management
Senior management is responsible for:
- Approving the information security policy
- Allocating adequate resources for information security
- Reviewing security performance quarterly

### 4.2 IT Department
The IT department shall:
- Implement technical security controls
- Maintain system security logs
- Respond to security incidents

### 4.3 Employees
All employees must:
- Complete annual security awareness training
- Report suspected security incidents
- Comply with acceptable use policies

## 5. Access Control
### 5.1 User Access Management
- User accounts shall be created based on business need
- Access rights shall be reviewed annually
- Terminated employee access shall be revoked within 24 hours

### 5.2 Password Requirements
- Passwords must be at least 8 characters long
- Passwords should contain a mix of letters and numbers
- Passwords shall not be shared

## 6. Physical Security
- Server rooms shall be locked and access restricted
- Visitor access shall be logged and escorted
- Workstations shall be locked when unattended

## 7. Incident Response
- Security incidents shall be reported to IT department
- Incident response team will investigate reported incidents
- Lessons learned will be documented after major incidents

## 8. Compliance
This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure continued effectiveness.

## 9. Policy Violations
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.
