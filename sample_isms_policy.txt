INFORMATION SECURITY MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this policy is to define the organizationâ€™s approach to protecting
information assets against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users who access
organizational information systems or data.

3. INFORMATION SECURITY OBJECTIVES
The organization aims to:
- Protect the confidentiality and integrity of business information
- Ensure availability of critical systems
- Reduce information security risks to an acceptable level

4. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
- Management is responsible for supporting information security initiatives.
- Employees must comply with security policies and procedures.
- The IT team is responsible for maintaining system security.

5. ASSET MANAGEMENT
The organization shall identify major information assets and ensure that systems
supporting critical business operations are protected.

6. ACCESS CONTROL
Access to information systems shall be restricted to authorized users.
User accounts shall be reviewed periodically.
Passwords must not be shared.

7. DATA PROTECTION
Sensitive data should be protected from unauthorized access.
Basic measures shall be taken to prevent data loss.

8. AWARENESS AND TRAINING
Employees shall receive information security awareness training during onboarding.

9. INCIDENT MANAGEMENT
Security incidents shall be reported to the IT department when identified.

10. COMPLIANCE
The organization shall comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements
related to information security.

11. POLICY REVIEW
This policy shall be reviewed periodically and updated as required.

12. ENFORCEMENT
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.
